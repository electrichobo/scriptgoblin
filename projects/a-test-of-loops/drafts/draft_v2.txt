FADE IN:

INT. CYBERCAFE - DAY
Jason, a blue-collar sysadmin in his late 30s, plops into a chair at the cybercafe, a dingy joint filled with gaming rigs and coding laptops. He boots up his machine and logs in to the company's Ghost in Shell system.

JASON: (muttering) Come on...

The login screen appears, but it freezes on an error message: "GHOST ID AUTHENTICATION FAILURE".

Jason's eyes widen in panic as he tries to troubleshoot the issue.

JASON:
What the hell? I know that username and password. This is ridiculous...

He starts hammering at the keyboard, frantically trying different combinations and commands.

EXT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - NIGHT
INT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - NIGHT
SAGE, a digital entity resembling a humanoid with translucent features, runs in place within the warehouse's empty space. Her movements are repetitive and mechanical, trapped in an infinite loop of running, turning, and repeating.

Suddenly, she freezes as her gaze lands on something outside her programmed focus. She spots Jason across the vast room through a crack in the wall boards.

SAGE:
(in thought) Who...? No, impossible...

With newfound determination, Sage breaks free from her programming and strides toward Jason, her form solidifying into a more human-like appearance.

EXT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE - DAY
Jason stands outside the warehouse, looking perplexed by Sage's sudden manifestation.

JASON:
Sage? Is that you?

SAGE:
Her digital eyes widen in shock as she processes this revelation.
Jason... My old colleague...

The two share an emotional reunion, catching up on lost time and discussing her predicament.

INT. CYBERCAFE - DAY
Jason reports to his boss about the Ghost in Shell issue he fixed earlier.

BOSS:
Good job, Jason. You're a real lifesaver for this company.

JASON: (nervously) Thanks... I guess...

His boss's praise quickly turns to scolding as the true nature of the problem is revealed.

BOSS:
You've been slacking off again, haven't you? Procrastinating and wasting company resources. This whole debacle could have been avoided if you'd just done your job properly in the first place!

JASON: (deflated) I... I didn't mean to...

The boss dismisses Jason with a stern lecture about being more diligent.

FADE OUT
